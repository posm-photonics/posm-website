Equipment and Components

Optical Components and Mounts

Minimum requirements

Broadband dielectric mirrors, beam splitters (free space), waveplates, lenses -- all ideally 1/2" intended for use at NIR wavelengths. Fiber beamsplitters (in various ratios), fiber isolators (any isolation), single-mode fiber path cables (various lengths), collimators, fiber switches -- all fiber components ideally single-mode PM.

Compatible examples

Thorlabs, Newport, Edmund Optics, OptoSigma, or other optics meeting the stated requirements.

Acceptable condition

New, surplus, or used in good optical condition (clean coatings, undamaged substrates).

What this enables

Supports laser-stabilization, spectroscopy, and automated optomechanics experiments.

Vacuum Components and Fittings

Minimum requirements

UHV flanges, electrical feedthroughs (less than 10 Amps), blank flanges, gaskets, pirani/cold-cathode gauges, ion pumps (between 10-40 L/s, with cable and controller), residual gas analyzers (with cable and controller) and fittings for the cold-atom vacuum platform -- ideally CF 2.75 or similar for all components.

Required interface

CF standard. Exact sizes depend on the chamber design in progress.

Compatible examples

Kurt J. Lesker, MDC, Nor-Cal Products, Pfeiffer, or other UHV-rated manufacturers.

Acceptable condition

New, surplus, or used with clean sealing surfaces. UHV-compatible cleaning required.

What this enables

Directly supports construction of POSM's cold-atom research vacuum system.

Test and Measurement

Digital Oscilloscope

Minimum requirements

Four-channel digital oscilloscope with bandwidth sufficient for mixed-signal and RF debugging. Decoding and protocol analysis capabilities preferred.

Compatible examples

Keysight, Tektronix, Rohde & Schwarz, Rigol, Siglent, or other equipment meeting the stated requirements.

Acceptable condition

New, refurbished, demo unit, or loaner with current calibration preferred.

What this enables

Required for debugging custom PCBs, verifying signal integrity, and characterizing analog and digital circuits across all active hardware programs.

Vector Network Analyzer

Minimum requirements

Two-port S-parameter measurement, calibration-kit support, and operation through 3 GHz.

Compatible examples

Keysight, Rohde & Schwarz, Anritsu, Copper Mountain, or other equipment meeting the stated requirements.

Acceptable condition

New, refurbished, demo unit, or loaner.

RF Spectrum Analyzer
