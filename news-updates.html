June 2026

Spectroscopy Lock, Vacuum Hardware, and Student Project Assignments

This month the team achieved our first spectroscopy lock using saturated absorption spectroscopy on rubidium. The vacuum system was assembled, baked, and brought to base pressure. Individual student project tracks were formally assigned.

Key milestones: lock acquisition and hold time characterization, ion gauge readout, initial servo loop tuning.

Laser Control Vacuum Team
May 2026

PCB Design Cycle Complete and RF Characterization Begins

The first custom PCB run returned from fabrication and was assembled. RF characterization of the servo electronics began this month, including gain-bandwidth product measurements and noise floor analysis.

RF / Electronics PCB
April 2026

Optics Table Build-Out and New Student Onboarding

The optical table was fully populated and aligned for the first time. Three new students joined the group and completed their initial orientation, safety training, and experimental onboarding.
