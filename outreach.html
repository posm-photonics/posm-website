Outreach.

POSM's mission extends beyond the lab. We are committed to training the next generation of experimental scientists and engineers through hands-on mentorship, workshops, and community engagement.

Training by doing.

At POSM, we believe the best way to learn experimental science is to actually do it — in a real lab, with real equipment, under real constraints. Our student-training model is at the heart of everything we do.

What we do

Our outreach pillars.

🧑‍🔬

Hands-On Research Experience

Students join active projects and immediately work with real equipment. We don't use toy setups — our platforms are research-grade, and the work is real.

🔗

Interdisciplinary Engineering

POSM intentionally brings together students from physics, EE, CS, and ME. Cross-disciplinary teams build better systems and better engineers.

🎓

Mentorship

Senior students mentor newer members on lab practices, hardware design, safety, and scientific communication. Mentorship flows in every direction.

🚀

Student Onboarding

We have a structured onboarding program covering lab safety, tool use, optics fundamentals, and project orientation so every new member gets up to speed fast.

📢

Demos and Workshops

We plan to host public demos, lab tours, and technical workshops for interested students, high schoolers, and community members as the group grows.

🌐

Talks and Presentations

Members present their work at university events, quantum technology forums, and student research showcases to build communication skills and visibility.
