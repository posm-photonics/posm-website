Lab Control

POSMq

Model-based lab control for experimental physics systems.

Connecting instruments, models, and experimental decisions.

Core Layers

Three layers, one platform.

01

Hardware State

Track settings, device states, and control parameters across an experiment.

02

Sensor Streams

Bring optical, electrical, and environmental signals into one monitoring layer.

03

Digital Models

Use model-based representations to support tuning, drift detection, and setup decisions.

Workflow

How it works.

Measure
Collect signals from instruments
Model
Compare to digital representations
Adjust
Recommend parameter changes
Monitor
Detect drift and system changes
Early Access

POSMq Early Access

POSMq is being developed as a model-based lab-control layer for experimental physics systems. We are interested in conversations with research groups, laboratories, and technical partners who may want to pilot future tools for system monitoring, tuning support, drift detection, and automated setup decisions.
