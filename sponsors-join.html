Sponsors.

POSM is a student-run group building serious experimental hardware. We rely on the generosity of the scientific and engineering community to accelerate our work.

Why it matters

We build real things.

Unlike simulation-only or purely theoretical groups, POSM students spend time in the lab designing, fabricating, and debugging physical systems. Your support goes directly into working hardware that trains the next generation of experimental scientists.

Students who work with POSM graduate with hands-on skills in optics, electronics, vacuum technology, control systems, and precision instrumentation — skills directly relevant to quantum technology companies, national labs, and research institutions.

How to Help

Ways to support POSM.

🔭

Equipment Donations

Surplus or loaned instruments — oscilloscopes, spectrum analyzers, lock-in amplifiers, optical components — go directly onto active experiments.

🔩

Parts & Component Donations

Optics, optomechanics, electronic components, connectors, cables, fasteners, and consumables are always needed and immediately useful.

💻

Software Licenses

EDA tools, CAD software, scientific computing licenses, and simulation tools help our students design faster and more effectively.

🧪

Loaner Instruments

Short-term loans of high-value test equipment allow us to characterize and validate systems that we couldn't otherwise access.

🏭

Fabrication Support

PCB manufacturing, assembly, CNC machining, and 3D printing partnerships accelerate our hardware development cycle.

👩‍🔬

Technical Mentorship

Industry engineers and scientists sharing expertise, reviewing designs, or visiting the lab provides invaluable guidance to our students.

💰

Financial Sponsorship

Direct financial contributions fund component purchases, conference registrations, lab consumables, and infrastructure upgrades.

Priority

Current needs.

The following categories represent our highest-priority needs as of the current semester. Reach out to discuss specific items.

Optics and Optomechanics
Mirrors, lenses, mounts, beam splitters, waveplates, posts, and breadboard accessories
RF and Microwave Equipment
Signal generators, spectrum analyzers, VNAs, cables, connectors, and attenuators
PCB Fabrication and Assembly
Fab partnerships, stencils, solder paste, rework equipment
Vacuum Hardware
Flanges, feedthroughs, gauges, pumps, and UHV-compatible components
Photonics Components
Fiber components, AOMs, EOMs, detectors, and photonic chip test fixtures
Test and Measurement
Oscilloscopes, lock-in amplifiers, power meters, and data acquisition systems

Ready to help?

We'd love to discuss how your organization can support POSM. Whether you have surplus equipment, software to offer, or want to explore financial sponsorship — reach out and we'll find the right way to work together.
