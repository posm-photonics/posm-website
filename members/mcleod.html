About

Prof. McLeod advises POSM and provides mentorship, lab space, and research direction. His interests include studying phase transitions in quantum matter and the opto-electronics of next-generation 2-dimensional materials including graphene, dichalcogenide semiconductors, and van der Waals heterostructures.
